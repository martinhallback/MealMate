OUR PHILOSOPHY

Students are looking for both affordable and nutritious food that is quick to prepare. With our offering, students have the opportunity to choose from different dishes at low prices that can be acquired swiftly. This promotes both students who feel they cook a little too much food for themselves, seeking an extra source of income and those who think they do not have time to cook their own. The vision for this idea is to increase the living standards of the student by making their daily life more efficient.

FAQ

How does pricing work?

Pricing for our lunchboxes is based on the selected options and any additional customizations. Our menu offers a variety of options at different price points to suit your budget.

How does pick-up work?

Pick-up for our lunchboxes is flexible and convenient. Once you place your order, you can select a preferred pick-up time from our available slots. Simply visit the given location at your chosen time to collect your freshly prepared meal.

Are your lunchboxes safe for individuals with allergies?

We take food safety seriously and strive to accommodate various dietary needs. Our lunchboxes are prepared in a controlled environment, and we take measures to prevent cross-contamination. However, please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order so we can take appropriate precautions.

What measures do you take to ensure food safety?

Food safety is our top priority. Our users follows strict hygiene practices, including regular handwashing and sanitization of cooking surfaces and utensils. We also monitor food temperatures to ensure proper storage and minimize the risk of foodborne illnesses.

Can I customize my lunchbox to suit my dietary preferences?

Yes, we offer customization options to accommodate various dietary preferences and restrictions. Whether you're vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or have other dietary needs, we can tailor your lunchbox to meet your requirements. Simply inform us of your preferences when placing your order.
